Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing and billing

Yes! You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time, no questions asked. When you cancel, you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period (either the end of your monthly cycle or annual term). Your subscription will simply not renew, and you'll be automatically downgraded to the Free tier.

All your data is preserved when you cancel Premium! If you have more than 5 subscriptions, they'll be in read-only mode until you either delete some subscriptions to get back under the 5-subscription limit, or resubscribe to Premium. Your data is never deleted unless you explicitly delete your account.

Yes! We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all Premium subscriptions (both monthly and annual). If you're not satisfied for any reason within 7 days of your purchase, you can request a full refund through the app or by emailing support@subscribely.app. After 7 days, standard subscription terms apply and no refunds are available, but you can still cancel anytime to prevent future charges.

Yes! You can upgrade from monthly to annual at any time to start saving immediately. When you switch to annual, we'll prorate your current monthly subscription and apply it to your first annual payment. Switching from annual to monthly is also possible, but you'll need to wait until your current annual term ends. Contact support@subscribely.app if you need help with plan changes.

For monthly subscriptions, you'll be charged immediately when you subscribe, then on the same date each month. For annual subscriptions, you'll be charged the full year upfront, then annually on your renewal date. You'll receive an email receipt for every charge, and you can view your billing history anytime in the app's Settings â†’ Subscription Management section.

If a payment fails, you'll receive an email notification immediately. We provide a 3-day grace period during which you'll retain Premium access and can update your payment method. You'll receive reminder emails on Day 1, Day 2, and Day 3. After the 3-day grace period, if payment still hasn't been received, you'll be automatically downgraded to the Free tier.
