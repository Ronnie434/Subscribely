Getting Started

Download the Subscribely app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Open the app and tap "Sign Up". Enter your email address and create a secure password. Verify your email by clicking the link we send you. That's it! You can now start tracking your subscriptions.

On the Home screen, tap the "+" button. Start typing the name of your subscription (e.g., "Netflix"). We'll auto-suggest popular services and fetch their logos. Enter the billing amount, select the frequency (monthly or yearly), set the renewal date, choose a category, and tap "Save". Your subscription is now tracked!

When adding or editing a subscription, toggle on "Renewal Reminders". The app will send you a notification 24 hours before the subscription renews. Make sure you've allowed notifications for Subscribely in your device settings (Settings â†’ Notifications â†’ Subscribely â†’ Allow Notifications).

All your subscriptions automatically sync to the cloud when you're logged in. Any changes you make on one device instantly appear on all your other devices. This works on both the Free and Premium tiers. Just make sure you're logged into the same account on all devices.
