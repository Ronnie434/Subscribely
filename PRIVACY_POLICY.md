# Privacy Policy for Smart Subscription Tracker

**Last Updated:** January 9, 2025

## Introduction

Smart Subscription Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Profile information (if provided)

### Subscription Data
The app stores information you provide about your subscriptions:
- Subscription names and descriptions
- Billing amounts and frequencies
- Renewal dates
- Categories
- Payment methods (names only, no credit card numbers)
- Custom notes

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App usage statistics (anonymized)
- Crash reports (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the subscription tracking service
- Send renewal notifications
- Sync your data across devices
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Provide customer support
- Ensure security and prevent fraud

## Data Storage and Security

### Supabase Backend
Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com), a trusted backend-as-a-service provider:
- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
- Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- Supabase complies with SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and CCPA standards
- Servers are located in secure, certified data centers

### Security Measures
We implement security measures including:
- Secure authentication using industry-standard protocols
- Encrypted data transmission
- Regular security audits
- Limited access to personal data

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We only share data with:
- **Supabase**: For secure data storage and authentication
- **Apple**: Basic app analytics (anonymized)
- **Google OAuth** (if you choose to sign in with Google): Only for authentication purposes

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data at any time through the app
- **Update** or correct your information
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your subscription data
- **Opt-out** of notifications

### Data Deletion
To delete your account and all data:
1. Open the app and go to Settings
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, email us at [your-email@example.com] to request account deletion.

## Data Retention

- Active account data is retained while your account is active
- Deleted accounts and data are permanently removed within 30 days
- Backup copies are deleted within 90 days

## Children's Privacy
